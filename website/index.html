Key Findings

A compact visual summary of the final project conclusions.

🌍
Geography dominates

The United States feature had the highest Random Forest importance score, showing how strong labor-market context is.

📈
Experience matters

Total coding experience and professional experience were among the strongest predictors of compensation.

💻
Language choice matters

ANOVA showed statistically significant salary differences across programming languages.

🎀
Popularity is limited

TIOBE popularity slightly improved Ridge Regression, but it is not a dominant salary signal on its own.
